There is a slightly positive correlation between the percentage of population between 25 and 29 and percentage of the population that says it is in excellent health.

This is likely due to the fact that this group is generally in or near their physical prime, and are just starting to begin their decline.

There is no real correlation between the total population in good health and the population between the ages of 25 and 29. There is a slight negative correlation between population of this age group and the percentage of the population in poor health.

There does appear to be a slight correlation between the percentage of the population that is male between 25 and 29 and the percentage of the population in good health.
